Landing page

Ambev

  • Client
    Ambev
  • Year
    2021
  • Role
    Product Designer

Introduction

Ambev, one of Brazil’s largest brewing companies, launched an internal initiative focused on recognizing and celebrating the efforts of its teams. As part of that, they needed a custom landing page to highlight all participants and winners of their company-wide contests, while also boosting engagement and creating a sense of connection across different departments.

I was responsible for the entire experience—from early discovery and ideation to final delivery—working within a tight timeline of just over a month and a half. The project required a balance of creative storytelling, interactivity, and technical execution.

The main objectives were to promote internal engagement, celebrate and recognize employees, create a personalized and immersive user journey, and deliver a visually impactful landing page built entirely with interactive 3D elements.

Process and Experience

I was responsible for the full design and development of the project. It began with quick interviews with Ambev’s leadership to align on goals and expectations. Right after that, I conducted a competitive analysis and explored code-based animation websites to gather visual and technical inspiration. This helped define the overall tone, interaction style, and creative direction of the landing page.

From there, I created the wireframes and mapped out the user flow, aiming for a smooth, engaging experience. All 3D animations and transitions were custom-built to bring energy and depth to the interface. I also implemented an employee ID login to make the experience more personalized.

Sound and motion played a key role throughout. Ambient audio added rhythm and emotion, while interactive elements encouraged users to explore contest categories and discover winners in a playful, rewarding way.



Experience Highlights

3D Animations:
I used custom 3D elements and smooth transitions to bring energy and movement into the interface, creating a sense of depth and visual flow as users navigated through the page

Sound Design:
Ambient audio layers were added to build rhythm and emotion, helping guide the user’s attention and making the experience feel more immersive and engaging.

Interactivity:
Users could explore contest categories, view winners, and interact with various animated elements—encouraging curiosity and rewarding exploration with playful responses.

The Finale:
To close the experience, I combined all elements—sound, movement, and interaction—into a cinematic ending. The camera moved in sync with the rhythm of the soundtrack, and messages appeared on-screen in time with the beat. This final interaction visually included the user in the moment, creating a strong sense of presence and turning the landing page into a memorable, emotionally resonant experience.

Conclusion

This project was a great opportunity to blend storytelling, design, and technology into a single immersive experience. In a short timeframe, I was able to bring Ambev’s internal initiative to life through thoughtful interactions, custom 3D animations, and emotional touchpoints. The result was a unique and engaging platform that not only celebrated employees but also strengthened their connection to the brand.
